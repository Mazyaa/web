The communication tool

The communication tool we designed and a mix of all your favorite apps in order to meet the criteria of all users.

With the latter you will be able to open threads of discussion with one or more people in order to create groups, transfer files of any format, launch calls in visio-coférences and many other features that we invite you to discover on Mazya.

The remuneration

You have now become a private teacher at Mazya and all work demands are due.

You will be paid according to the number of times you give your client, according to this scheme: 1 hour equals 1 euro. But it’s not just that you can also earn bonuses depending on your customer’s satisfaction.

Your customers will be able to rate you on a scale of 10. Of course, the closer you get to the maximum score, the bigger your bonus at the end of the month will be.

This rating function does not just allow us to reward you but also allows us to see who does not have the abilities to be on Mazya as a private teacher.
